History

TechCon began in 2010 as a grassroots gathering of developers and tech enthusiasts. Over the years, it has grown into a global platform for innovation, collaboration, and inspiration.

Mission

Our mission is to empower the tech community by fostering knowledge exchange, celebrating innovation, and promoting inclusive growth across all sectors of technology.

Past Speakers

TechCon has hosted some of the most influential voices in tech:

  • Dr. Aisha Mburu

    AI researcher and advocate for ethical machine learning. Her keynote in 2022 focused on bias in algorithms.

  • Kenji Tanaka

    Founder of QuantumLeap Systems. He shared insights on quantum computing breakthroughs in 2021.

  • Maria Gonzalez

    Cybersecurity expert and author. Her 2020 session on digital privacy remains one of the most viewed talks.
